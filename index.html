Privacy Policy

These are our offical statments regarding privacy in the use of applications (Board Game Banker, Crossfire Lotto Numbers) and subsequent websites related to the applications.

Applications free editions use Googles AdMob advertising service to support developers. AdMobs Privacy Policy is available at http://www.admob.com/home/privacy. None of the applications use AdMobs optional GPS/Location based ad targeting.

Applications also use Google Analytics to gather traffic information related to development of the applications. This may include (Screen Views, Button Clicks, Ad Loads, and Application Performance(Measured thru system tim in (ms)). Google Analytics privacy policy is available at http://www.google.com/intl/en/analytics/privacyoverview.html

All information collected for our use is not shared with anyone. we do not release any information collected with out a court order. Please contact at benrcoulston@gmail.com with any questions
